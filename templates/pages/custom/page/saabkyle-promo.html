Contest Giveaway

Second Skin Audio and Saabkyle04 are teaming up to give away 10 Damplifier Pro sound deadening packs to Saabkyle04 viewers. The prize pool gets bigger the more entries we receive, so please share with your friends and fellow car enthusiasts! Winners’ vehicles will be featured in a future Saabkyle04 YouTube video. We look forward to highlighting your projects on the channel!

Entry Details

Contestants can enter the giveaway by joining an email list (1 entry), tweeting about the contest (1 entry), or referring a friend (1 entry per referral, up to 10). Limit of 1 door pack per person. For every 500 entries, we’ll add 5 Door Packs to the giveaway. You must be 16 with a valid US driver’s license and address to enter. The contest ends at midnight on July 14th.

Prize Details

Each Damplifier Pro Door Pack contains 8 sheets (13.5 Sq Ft) of premium sound deadener and a wooden hand roller for easy application. Each winner will be chosen at random using a random number generator. Winners are asked to install the products within 30 days and then share pictures or videos of their install to Saabkyle or Second Skin via email or social media. Winners will be contacted via email and have seven days to claim the prize or a new winner will be selected.
