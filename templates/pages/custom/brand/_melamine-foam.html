Melamine foam is a highly absorptive open cell foam used in cars to improve acoustics and add thermal insulation. Best known as the super-absorptive magic eraser, high quality versions of melamine foam are aerospace grade materials. Instead of hand-held sponges, these enhanced versions can be bought as melamine foam sheets and have exceptional physical properties. Because the sheets are hydrophobic, they are safe to use in vehicles, allowing car owners to choose between open cell vs closed cell foam.

Second Skin’s Mega Zorbe hydrophobic melamine foam sheets come with a peel and stick adhesive or with no adhesive included. These sheets will add insulation to a car’s roof above the headliner or improve speaker acoustics and reduce echoes when installed in door cavities.
